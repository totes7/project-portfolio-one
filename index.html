Basketball Youth Tournament

Brighton seafront & preston park

About Us

First edition of the BYT in Brighton,UK. Here to help the basketball community to grow and have a good time!

Selection

If your aged 14-16yo, come down to Preston Park and register for the tournament. 320 spots available, so make sure to get in line early!

Teams

We'll create 32 teams of 10 players each. The team creation process will be completely randomized to ensure fairness.

Winner

Teams will be divided into 8 groups of 4, every team in a group will play each other twice. The two teams with the most wins advance to the playoffs, which will be played like a NBA conference playoff.
