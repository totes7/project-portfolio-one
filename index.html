About Us

Second edition of the BYT in Brighton,UK. Here once again to help the East Sussex basketball community to grow and have a good time! Our values are the same as last year, bringing Brighton youth closer togheter through the beautiful game of basketball.

Selection

If your aged 14-16yo, register and come down to Preston Park for the Selection Camp. 320 spots available, so make sure to get in shape!

Teams

We'll create 32 teams of 10 players each. The team creation process will be completely randomized to ensure fairness. Each team will select their own captain and choose their own name.

Winner

Teams will be divided into 8 groups of 4, every team in a group will play each other twice. The two teams with the most wins advance to the playoffs, which will be played like a NBA conference playoff.
